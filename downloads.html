How to report a Cybercrime in Australia (Victoria)?

If you experience cybercrime in Victoria, report it through these official channels.

  • Report to ACSC - Report cyber threats and incidents.
  • Report to Victoria Police - Report online fraud, cybercrime, and related offenses.
  • Report Scams to ScamWatch - Report phishing and online scams.
